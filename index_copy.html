Register For A Hero Arm

The Hero Arm is an advanced and affordable multi-grip bionic arm for children and adults with below elbow upper-limb differences, aged 8 and above. It's currently available through prosthetics clinics in the USA, UK, Ireland, France, Spain, Portugal, Australia, New Zealand, Netherlands and Russia. Register your interest now and we'll refer you to your nearest prosthetics clinic. Your bionic journey begins here.
